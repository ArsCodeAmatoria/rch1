## Documentation intent

Inspection documentation must demonstrate control integrity, not just checklist completion. Records should support operational decisions and equipment traceability.

<DiagramFigure title="Inspection flow">
  <p>Pre-use verification -> Defect classification -> Corrective action -> Return-to-service authorization.</p>
</DiagramFigure>

## Required record fields

- Equipment ID, type, and rating
- Inspector identity and competency basis
- Findings with defect severity classification
- Disposition status and corrective action references

<WarningBox>
  <p>All rigging hardware must be inspected before use and removed from service when rejection criteria are met.</p>
</WarningBox>

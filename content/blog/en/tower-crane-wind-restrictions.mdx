## Operational baseline

Prior to hoisting operations, wind conditions must be verified against current manufacturer limits and project-specific control thresholds. Wind management is not a static pre-check; it is a continuous operating control.

<WarningBox>
  <p>Crane operators must confirm live wind readings before each high-exposure lift sequence.</p>
  <p>No personnel shall enter suspended load zones during wind-triggered hold conditions.</p>
</WarningBox>

## Field procedure

<ProcedureBox>
  <ol>
    <li>Verify anemometer function and confirm reading location relevance to jib exposure.</li>
    <li>Apply reduced operating envelope for high sail-area loads.</li>
    <li>Issue stop-work when gust behavior exceeds defined thresholds.</li>
    <li>Secure crane in out-of-service configuration where required.</li>
  </ol>
</ProcedureBox>
